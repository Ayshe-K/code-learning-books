# Why Mrs. Lane Needs to Create a Gist

Oh, Mrs. Lane. Bless your heart. You've never even *opened* GitHub before, have you?

## What's a Gist?
A Gist is like a Post-It note, but for code. It's GitHub's way of letting you share snippets without creating a whole repository.

## Why Do You Need One?
1. **Training wheels** - You're not ready for a full repo, Mrs. Lane.
2. **Show your work** - Like holding up your notebook, but digital.
3. **Peer pressure** - Everyone's doing it.

## How to Create a Gist
1. Go to github.com
2. Click + in top right
3. Select "New gist"
4. Paste your code
5. Click "Create public gist"

## Pro Tip
If you create it without closing your browser 17 times, you win at life.
